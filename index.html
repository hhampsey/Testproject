MY HOBBY

How I got into Gardening

Gardening is something that has always been an interest in my family. No one in my family had a "Green Thumb" or anything, but my family always appreciated the beauty of flowers. We may have dabbled in growing vegetables and herbs, but our main focus was always flowers. Any time someone hears "I enjoy gardening" people assume I have a little vegetable garden or herb garden. Just flowers...

I got into gardening when I was older and in college. My grandma and mom had always enjoyed flowers and one day I was helping them out with planting flowers and realized how relaxing it is. Everyone talks about how important it is to be mindful, to be IN the moment. No one really talks about how easy it can be to be mindful or IN the moment when you truly are interested in what you’re doing and enjoying the company around you. What makes mindfulness difficult is trying to force ourselves to engage in something that we are just not interested in. For me, Gardening is a great way to relax and enjoy nature. It allows me to connect with the earth. I never had large plots of land where I planted flowers, most of the time, they were window sill pots and flowerpots I found at random garage sales. That’s the nice thing about flowers; they look beautiful wherever you put them. As long as you take care of them, they will continue to look beautiful until they die.

I often say, sometimes a plain cheese pizza just hits the spot

Sometimes people miss out on how beautiful simplicity can be


BENEFITS OF GARDENING

Physical Wellness Movement of the body is important for everyone. Gardening is a great way to get exercise without even realizing it. You are moving your arms, legs, and back while you are digging in the dirt, planting flowers, and watering them. It’s a great way to get exercise without even realizing it.

Mental Health Wellness Gardening is a great way to relieve stress. It allows you to be in the moment and focus on something other than your worries.

Social Wellness Gardening can be a great way to connect with others. You can join a gardening club or just talk to your friends and family about it. It's a great way to bond with others and share your love of flowers. However, personally it has brought me closer to my mom by going flower shopping together. It brought me closer to my fiancee's best friend as he is a landscaper and has helped me navigate my garden; even helping out with the heavy lifting!
